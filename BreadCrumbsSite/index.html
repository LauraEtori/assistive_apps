BreadCrumbs uses the Apple ARKit, utilizing vision processing and onboard sensors to track your phone as you move around.

BreadCrumbs doesn't use GPS, so it can work with high accuracy even in buildings and underground.

You can be part of the experiment. We are constantly trying to improve the experience and would be happy to hear your feedback.

Find Your Seat Again

BreadCrumbs can help you navigate back through an unfamiliar movie theater, restaurant, or office building.

Find Your Way There

BreadCrumbs will record your route with the press of a button.

Do Your Business

Find Your Way Back

From Users to Users

BreadCrumbs was designed from the ground up to assist people who are blind.

More than 30% of our development team are visually impaired, and we have worked closely with users to develop a seamless experience.

BreadCrumbs is completely compatible with VoiceOver and designed with accessibility and usability in mind.

We are not done; we need your help to make the experience even better.

AR Meets Assistive Tech

We're bringing together assistive technology and augmented reality to navigate you back through unfamiliar areas.
Augmented reality, exemplified by Apple's ARKit, superimposes virtual elements onto the real world. In this case, we place virtual "keypoints" in real locations as you move.

How It Works

  • As you walk to a location, your phone keeps track of its path with a series of virtual crumbs.
  • After you get there, your phone simplifies the path to a series of "keypoints" where you made a turn or used a stairway.
  • As you navigate back, your phone will give you instructions to each point, in reverse order, until you reach your starting location again.
  • You're now back at your seat, classroom, or restaurant table. Enjoy yourself.
